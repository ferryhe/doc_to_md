# Mathpix Handwritten Formula Reference

This reference Markdown was prepared for a handwritten-formula benchmark built from official Mathpix example images.

## Sample 1

$$
\lim_{x \to 3}\left(\frac{x^2 + 9}{x - 3}\right)
$$

$$
\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}\cos n\theta \cos(x \sin\theta)\, d\theta
